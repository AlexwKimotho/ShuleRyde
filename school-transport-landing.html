Safety First

Built around your child's safety

Every policy, every feature, and every driver is held to the highest standard. Safety isn't an add-on — it's the foundation.

Background-Verified Drivers
Every driver undergoes a full DCI background check, NTSA license verification, and our in-house child safety training before their first route.
NTSA Compliant Fleet
All vehicles are inspected, insured, and carry valid NTSA certification. Speed limiters, seat belts, and fire extinguishers are mandatory on every van.
Real-Time Route Monitoring
Our operations team watches every active route in real time. If a van deviates or stops unexpectedly, we notify parents and dispatch support immediately.
